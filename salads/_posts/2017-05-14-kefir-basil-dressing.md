##### Ingredients:

* 1 bottle kefir
* 1 handful basil
* 1 tablespoon minced garlic
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 pinch pepper

<br>

##### Directions:

1. Blend until smooth in a blender
2. Bottle and save for up to 3 days
