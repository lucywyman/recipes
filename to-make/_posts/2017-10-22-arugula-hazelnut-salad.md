##### Ingredients:

* 1 cup arugula, coarsely chopped
* 1 cup mixed greens, coarsely chopped
* 1/2 cup pea sprouts
* 1/2 cup hazelnuts

##### For the dressing:
* 1 teaspoon olive oil
* 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
* 1/2 teaspoon honey
* 1/2 teaspoon salt

<br>

##### Directions:

1. Chop ingredients
2. Mix dressing ingredients in a small bowl
3. Pour dressing over greens and toss

Serve!
