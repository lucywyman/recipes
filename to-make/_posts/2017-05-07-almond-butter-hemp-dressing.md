#### Ingredients:

* 2 dates, pitted
* ⅔ cup hot water
* 2 heaping tablespoons red miso
* ¼ cup almond butter
* 2 tablespoons hemp seeds
* 1 clove garlic, peeled
* 2 teaspoons minced ginger

<br>

#### Directions:

1. Pulse all ingredients together in a blender until smooth. 
2. Cover and chill for 10 minutes.
