    Maple Oatmeal with Sweet Potato


    From: Foodie Crush

    Ingredients:

    • ½ cup oatmeal, old fashioned or quick
    • ⅔ cup non-fat milk, or low-fat if you prefer
    • pinch of salt
    • about ⅓ cup cooked sweet potato, mashed
    • 1-2 teaspoons brown sugar
    • pumpkin pie spice
    • maple syrup
    • chopped almonds


    Directions:

    1. Mix the oats, milk, salt and sweet potato in a microwave safe bowl and cook on high for 1 minute 30 seconds.
    2. Remove from the microwave and sprinkle with brown sugar and a dash or two of pumpkin pie spice and stir.
    3. Drizzle with maple syrup, add a small handfull of chopped almonds and add more milk if desired. Serve warm.
