    Baked Sweet Potato Fries


    Total time: 20 minutes

    From: Skinny Taste

    Ingredients:
    • 2 medium sweet potatoes, washed
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • paprika to taste
    • salt and fresh pepper to taste


    Directions:
    1. Preheat oven to 425°F and spray a cooking sheet with non-stick spray.
    2. Slice each potato into ½ inch thick strips.
    3. Toss with oil, salt, pepper and paprika.
    4. Spread in single-layer on the baking sheet, bake for 10 minutes.
    5. Turn oven to broil and bake for another 7-10 minutes, until crispy and golden brown
