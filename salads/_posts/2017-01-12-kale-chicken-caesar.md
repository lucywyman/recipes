#### Ingredients:

* 1 large bag kale
* ¼ cup dressing of choice
* ¼ cup pepitas
* 1 medium chicken breast, cubed
* 2 tbsp olive oil

<br>

#### Directions:
1. Sauté the chicken in the olive oil until browned on all sides, about 10 minutes
2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and enjoy!
